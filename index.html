whoAmI?

Tiarama, that's what I call myself online. It's always been fun to play with an alter ego. I mean... Craig MacLeod is such a masculine Scottish name isn't it? Fair enough I am half Scottish and I do enjoy a deep fried mars bar as much as the next person (who doesn't?), but I'm also half Filipino and really not that masculine.

whatIsIt?

This is my webpage. It's really for myself: to build on as my coding improves and to showcase new skills as I learn them. It's fun putting things like this together, but it gives you an idea of my current level too. You must be curious or you wouldn't be here, right? ♥

why.

Everybody, not forgetting you, has been through times where we've needed to make big, scary changes to our lives. This is one of those time's for me. The tech universe has always provoked a fascination and an excitement to learn in myself, so I decided I would finally be brave enough to hold my breath, close my eyes, and throw myself into the water. Admittedly I'm terrified, but I think that's part of the fun.
